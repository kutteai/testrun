Troubleshooting Steps

Step 1: Check Chrome version at chrome://version/ - you need Chrome 88+
Step 2: Go to chrome://extensions/ and check if PayCio Wallet is enabled
Step 3: Click "Details" on PayCio Wallet and check "Site access" is set to "On all sites"
Step 4: Click "Service Worker" and check for errors in the console
Step 5: Try disabling other extensions temporarily
Step 6: Test in incognito mode
